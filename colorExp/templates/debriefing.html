The purpose of this experiment is to examine the relationship between shape names, shape properties (pointedness or roundedness), and color variations in the choice buttons. In this experiment, we manipulated all three of these variables and will build cognitive models that predict choices based on these variables.

Thank you for assisting with our research. Your results will be combined with other participants to help advance the state of scientific knowledge. If you would like to learn more, please contact Hunter Leise at hunter.leise@colorado.edu.
